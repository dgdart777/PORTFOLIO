BIOLOGICAL INFORMATION

Dheepana Gopi Devi Nirhoo is a final-year student at the University of South Africa (UNI​​SA), where creativity and curiosity drive an exploration of the human experience through various art forms. 

With a love for carving, painting, sketching, and digital art, Dheepana delves into the intricacies of human emotions, aiming to create a deeper understanding of these through each piece.

Alongside personal work, Dheepana also contributes to the community, volunteering artwork to raise funds for charitable organisations and designing magazine covers that merge art with purpose.
